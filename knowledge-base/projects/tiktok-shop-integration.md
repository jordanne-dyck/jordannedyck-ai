# TikTok Shop Integration

## Executive Summary

Led The Ordinary's launch on TikTok Shop as **the first fully integrated brand** on the platform via Salesforce Commerce Cloud. Delivered end-to-end integration in 6 months, creating what is now a **$30M+ annual revenue channel** with significant first-mover advantage in social commerce.

**Key Achievement**: Worked alongside Salesforce and TikTok to refine beta Salesforce Commerce Cloud integration for production, becoming first  brand with full technical integration (not just catalog upload) and setting foundation for social commerce at DECIEM.

## The Problem & Opportunity

- TikTok Shop emerging as major social commerce platform with explosive growth
- The Ordinary products going viral organically on TikTok

  - Real-time inventory sync
  - Automated order routing
  - Price and promotion sync
  - Unified customer service

## Challenges & Learnings

### What Worked Well

✅ **Early partner engagement**: Beta program access gave competitive edge
✅ **Custom integration**: Fully automated vs. manual workarounds, scales perfectly

### Challenges Overcome

**Building Without a Roadmap**
- Problem: First-mover means no documentation, no best practices, no reference implementations
- Solution: Direct partnership with TikTok Shop team, learned by building, documented our own patterns
- Result: Created the template others now follow, became TikTok's showcase partner

**Betting on Unproven Platform**
- Problem: Significant engineering investment in platform with uncertain future (political risk, market adoption risk)
- Solution: Framed as strategic learning vs. all-in commitment, kept integration modular for reuse
- Result: Even with platform uncertainty, gained valuable social commerce capabilities and first-mover advantage

## Skills Demonstrated

**Product Strategy**: Identified social commerce opportunity, built business case, prioritized over competing initiatives

**Technical Architecture**: Designed scalable integration connecting two platforms with minimal documentation, no existing patterns

**First Principles Thinking**: Chose custom integration when no proven path existed, built the template

**Vendor Management**: Partnered with TikTok Shop adn Salesforce team, influenced platform roadmap, became showcase partner

**Risk Management**: Phased regional rollout, extensive testing, monitoring infrastructure minimized launch risk

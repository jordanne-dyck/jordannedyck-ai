# Regimen Builder

## Executive Summary

Built an interactive, rules-based skincare regimen builder that guides customers through creating personalized multi-step routines tailored to their specific skin concerns and goals. Achieved 35% higher conversion rate and 40% higher average order value compared to standard product browsing.

**Key Innovation**: Translated complex dermatological knowledge into an intuitive tool that democratizes expert skincare advice at scale. Later evolved into AI-powered Unified Personal Shopper system.

## The Problem & Opportunity

**The Challenge:**
- Customers overwhelmed by choice: "Which products for my concerns?", "What order?", "What can't be mixed?"
- High cart abandonment
- Low average order value (customers buying single products vs. full routines)
- Low retention rate
- 40% of customer service inquiries about product selection

**Business Impact:**
- Lost revenue: Customers buying 1-2 products when they need 5-7 for complete routine
- Poor customer experience: Frustration, negative reviews
- $200K+ annually in customer service for product advice

**Strategic Insight:**
Customers want The Ordinary's efficacy and pricing, but need guidance to navigate complexity. Traditional product pages don't solve this. Opportunity: Replicate in-store expert consultation digitally.

## Solution Design

Build UI that takes customers through a simple quiz to understand their skin type, concerns, and goals → recommend complete routine with products in right order and usage instructions using rules-based recommendation engine on website front end.


## Results & Impact

- 35% higher conversion rate
- 40% higher average order value
- Reduction in "which product" customer service inquiries
- Higher quality reviews (customers understood products better)
- Proved value of guided shopping experiences

## Challenges & Learnings

### What Worked Well

✅ **Customer-centric design**: Extensive research and iteration created intuitive experience
✅ **Lab team partnership**: Expert input ensured accuracy and credibility

### Challenges Overcome

**Encoding Expert Knowledge**
- Problem: Translating nuanced scientific knowledge into algorithmic rules
- Solution: Partnered with in-house lab, legal, regulatory and brand to document compatibility matrix, created fallback logic
- Result: Inputs generated reasonable routines, edge cases handled gracefully

**Replicating In-Store Expert Consultation**
- Problem: Tool needed to feel as close to talking to knowledgeable store associate as possible, balancing scientific accuracy with user-friendly simplicity
- Solution: Collaborated with retail team, captured their language patterns and customer education approach, translated first-hand customer interactions into digital experience, iterative testing
- Result: High completion rate despite complex topic

## Skills Demonstrated

**Product Management**: Identified customer pain point, built business case, defined requirements, prioritized features

**UX/UI Design**: Designed intuitive quiz flow, created visual routine builder, iterated based on user testing

**Customer Research**: Conducted interviews, analyzed support data, validated designs

**Technical Product Leadership**: Translated scientific knowledge into algorithmic logic, designed rules engine

**Cross-Functional Collaboration**: Partnered with lab, legal, regulatory, brand, engineers, marketing, customer service, retail

**Data-Driven Iteration**: A/B tested variations, analyzed drop-off, optimized based on metrics

## Evolution to AI

**2024: Next Generation (AI-Powered)**

This rules-based tool laid foundation for the **Unified Personal Shopper** AI agent system:

**What Stayed:**
- Core problem (customer confusion, need for guidance)
- Routine architecture principles and compatibility knowledge
- Educational approach and "why" explanations

**What Evolved:**
- Rules engine → Multi-agent AI system
- Fixed quiz → Natural language conversation
- Static recommendations → Dynamic, personalized suggestions
- One-time tool → Ongoing relationship (post-purchase support, reordering)

**Key Learning:**
The regimen builder proved customers value expert guidance at scale. AI scaled the concept from rules-based tool to conversational experience.

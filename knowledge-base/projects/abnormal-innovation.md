# Abnormal Innovation - Internal Strategy & Innovation Lab

## Executive Summary

Founded Abnormal Innovation without a mandate to do so. Identified a gap — DECIEM was scaling fast but teams were chasing the next shiny thing without focus, pouring effort into initiatives that hadn't been validated. No framework for deciding what was worth building vs. what was a distraction. Made the case, built MVPs to prove the model, and scaled it into DECIEM's internal digital strategy and innovation lab. Created the discipline to validate ambiguous opportunities quickly and scale only what proved value — killing everything else before it consumed resources.

**Why "Abnormal"**: Reflects DECIEM's brand DNA — challenging conventions, thinking differently, and not being constrained by how things are "supposed" to be done.

## The Lab's Purpose

### The Problem We Solve

As DECIEM scaled rapidly (80% YoY growth), organizational complexity increased:
- **Siloed teams** working on overlapping problems without awareness
- **Conflicting priorities** and lack of strategic alignment across departments
- **Analysis paralysis** on major decisions due to too many stakeholders
- **Innovation bottleneck** - great ideas getting stuck in execution
- **Technical debt** from rapid growth without strategic oversight
- **Resource inefficiency** - teams reinventing wheels or duplicating efforts

**The Need**: Internal consultancy that moves fast, thinks strategically, facilitates alignment, and delivers solutions—without the overhead and misalignment that external consultants often create.

### Our Operating Model

**Entrepreneurial Approach:**
We operate like an internal startup:
- Identify opportunities through pattern recognition
- Test hypotheses rapidly
- Scale what works
- Kill what doesn't
- Focus on disruption and white space for innovation

**How We Work:**

**1. Intake**: Teams come to us with problems, opportunities, or strategic questions

**2. Framing**: We reframe the challenge, identify root causes, define success metrics

**3. Sprint Planning**: Structure work into focused sprints with clear deliverables

**4. Collaboration**: Work embedded with teams, not in isolation

**5. Delivery**: Ship solutions, frameworks, or strategic recommendations

**6. Measurement**: Track outcomes and iterate

## Our Principles

**1. Pattern Recognition Over Process**
- Don't follow rigid playbooks
- Identify patterns across problems
- Apply learnings from one domain to another
- Creative problem-solving over checklist execution

**2. Bias for Action**
- Test > Theorize
- Ship MVPs fast, iterate based on data
- Fail fast, learn faster
- Prototypes over PowerPoints

**3. Outcome-Driven, Not Output-Driven**
- Measure success by business impact, not deliverables or features
- Focus on moving key metrics
- Kill projects that aren't working

**4. Strategic Thinking + Hands-On Execution**
- Don't just create strategies—build and ship
- Technical capability to execute (not just advise)
- Balance strategic vision with tactical implementation

**5. Facilitate Alignment, Don't Dictate**
- Build consensus through workshops and collaboration
- Make decisions with data and frameworks
- Empower teams to own outcomes

**6. Challenge the Status Quo**
- Question assumptions
- Push for innovation and disruption
- "This is how we've always done it" is not an acceptable reason
- Embrace the "Abnormal"
